Established estate agents Sam Cooke and Jamie Curtis launch a new offering for Cambridge. In a buoyant, yet crowded, marketplace under pressure from online disruptors they played to their strength: authentic brand narrative infuses every channel from signage to social media; straightforward in its approach, cutting through the usual rhetoric of an industry guilty of unhelpful euphemisms, and self-aware, meeting pre-conceptions of estate agents head on.

The monotone visual identity differentiates from the ubiquity of corporate plus accent colour palettes and the typographic approach balances established integrity with a dynamic newness. In three years since opening their doors Cooke Curtis & Co have increased their team to 15 full-time staff, added a lettings division and become the biggest selling agent in South Cambridge (according to Rightmove). Their ongoing appreciation of the importance of brand will soon see new branches opening across the city.
