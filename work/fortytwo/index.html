Named for Douglas Adams’s answer to the meaning of life, the universe and everything (in The Hitchhiker’s Guide to the Galaxy), 42evolution is a project funded by the Sedgwick Museum that reaches out to a universal audience to educate on the breadth of thinking within the study of evolution. 42 filmed interviews with prominent evolutionists such as Simon Conway Morris and Sir David Attenborough offer a world class resource to all audiences with an interest in the subject, both within and beyond academia.
