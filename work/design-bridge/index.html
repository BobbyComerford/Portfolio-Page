Global brand agency Design Bridge sought to manifest their brand in a digital context. Whilst the appointed company required strong design and development skills, what was most important was an ability to empathise with the Chief Creative Officer and his team, to understand and interpret their creative vision for the agency. In a series of sessions, way before we began to design and code, the way Design Bridge used language, shot work and delivered content was explored. The aim was to establish what made them who they are.

The team were photographed in places across London that meant something to them personally, project imagery was conceptual and painstakingly art directed, and words were artfully crafted. This work provided the blueprint for how Design Bridge communicate moving forward. The resulting website is a perfect expression of this ferocious and friendly agency. Since its launch Design Bridge have seen rapid and continued growth and latterly have been acquired by creative behemoth WPP.
