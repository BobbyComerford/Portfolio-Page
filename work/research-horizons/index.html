The University of Cambridge publishes a quarterly review of its world-leading research output, its contents never less than fascinating, ground-breaking and challenging to conventional thinking. Research Horizons is an organ which requires an inspirational design treatment.

An article-specific, conceptual approach to headlines and illustration married to clean, simple and understated typography in its content design creates impact and draws in casual browsers. The iconic masthead has become a recognisable sight in the entrance areas of all university faculties, colleges and institutes across the city and the magazine enjoys a strong and devoted national and international readership.
