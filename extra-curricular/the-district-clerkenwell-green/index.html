The District, London.

The District

We love Cambridge; the people, the architecture, the access to intellectually challenging projects make it a very special place to live and work. But as we’ve grown, so has the demand from clients who call London home, so in January 2019 we will officially open The District, Clerkenwell Green, London. Welcome to our Second Home.

It was very important to us that we found a space that was representative of our ideology and Second Home’s mission to support creativity and entrepreneurship really resonated with us, as did their cultural programmes, which include talks on subjects ranging from human centred design to the refugee crisis to gourmet sardine tasting. We look forward to meeting our eclectic neighbours.

We now live at The Gatehouse, 23 – 25 Gwydir Street, Cambridge, CB1 2LG and 45 – 47 Clerkenwell Green, London, EC1R 0EB.
