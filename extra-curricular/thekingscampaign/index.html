The King’s Campaign.

The District

We were thrilled to be appointed to work with King’s College Cambridge on their recently launched fundraising campaign. The project begins with the development of an identity that captures the progressive and transformative nature of the college and is being applied to a range of off and online communications. The central concept uses the copy device ‘The Making Of’ to reflect both the character building nature of their culture and the many impacts their alumni have had, and continue to have on the world. The project will be added in full to the website soon.
