Antony Gormley ‘SUBJECT’ Kettle’s Yard.

The District

We recently enjoyed ‘SUBJECT’, an arresting show capturing many of Antony Gormley’s interests, including how sculpture can activate both the space that it occupies and the body of the viewer. Offering a series of physical and metaphysical encounters, it also celebrated the newly proportioned space that Kettle’s Yard now offers. The exhibition includes the first in a new series of works, Subject (2018), from which the title of the show derives, and the first UK showing of Infinite Cube II (2018), made of one-way mirror glass and 1,000 LED lights.
